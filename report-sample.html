Report Sample

Front cover

We take pride in providing you with quality information. We understand that you are seeking information about possibly your greatest asset.

About your property

The information will include technical data about the property itself. It will include lot size, dimensions, zoning and title details. This is so we can be sure it is the correct property and we take into account all the features of the property.

Nearby properties sold

The report will include properties sold in in your area. This is an essential part of assessing what buyers are paying for similar properties in your area. This provides information that is fact not opinion.

Suburb statistics

Apart from being interesting this information can often help in directing marketing so that any advertising will appeal to the correct demographic and likely selling or renting public.

Nearby rentals

When renting property it is important to be aware of what is being asked for rent at other properties in your area. This is your competition and your likely rental return could be influenced by this competition.

Nearby properties for sale

Although asking prices of other homes may not actually represent the actual selling price it is important to take into account your competition when selling. This is why we provide this information for you.

Appraisal price range

Finally your report will state a likely price range of the sale price for your property today.
