Suburb Profile: Trinity Park

Nearby Suburbs

  • Trinity Beach
  • Smithfield
  • Kewarra Beach

Trinity Park is located on the coast north of Cairns and just south of Trinity Beach. The wetlands in the area have significant environmental value, and are precious given the high levels of development that have occurred and are continuing to occur on the remainder of the City's northern beaches. Trinity Park is about 25mins drive north of Cairns CBD.

The People

The population of the area is relatively small at 915, which is characterised by families with young children. There is a major development with marina facilities in this suburb. Other parts of this area have lower-cost homes. 

General facilities

Trinity Park has only a few community facilities and services, including the Krait Street Park. The nearest library and swimming pool are at Smithfield, about 6mins drive south. Other services can be found at Trinity Beach, about 6mins drive north.

Medical

Trinity Beach, about 6mins drive north, has a medical centre with a general practitioner. There are a number of medical, dental and pathology services in the nearby suburb of Smithfield, about 8mins drive south. The closest hospital is in Cairns city, about 20mins drive south.

Shopping

The Campus Shopping Centre is right next to the suburb of Trinity Park. There is a small convenience shopping centre at Trinity Beach, about 6mins drive north, with a number of takeaway food outlets, a newsagent, a butcher, a bakery and a chemist. There is also a hair salon. The nearest major shopping centre is at Smithfield, about 8mins drive south.

Childcare

There is no child care in Trinity Park, but Johnny Crowe's Child Care Centre is halfway between this suburb and Trinity Beach. Other options include the suburbs of Smithfield and Kewarra Beach.

School

There is a Catholic primary school in Trinity Park and Smithfield State High School is only minutes from the suburb. There is a state primary school in Trinity Beach. 

Public transport

Sunbus provides a public transport service to the area and taxis are available.
