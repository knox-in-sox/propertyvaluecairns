Suburb Profile: Palm Cove

Nearby Suburbs

  • Clifton Beach
  • Ellis Beach

Palm Cove is one of the northern most beaches in the Cairns area; it is about 35mins drive north of Cairns CBD. It is increasingly characterised by tourist accommodation, resorts, and restaurants. The beach has a stinger net enclosure and a jetty. Palm Cove is one of the best suburbs in Cairns with highly sought after, high quality accommodation options. It is considered to be an 'exclusive' suburb.

The People

The population of Palm Cove consists mainly of high wage earners and retirees with a transient population of visitors and hospitality workers.

General facilities

 Palm Cove has few 'community' facilities and services, apart from the stinger-net enclosure on the beachfront; but it does have a large number of user pays facilities and services. The nearest library and public swimming pool are at Smithfield, about 20mins drive south.

Medical

There are no medical services in Palm Cove but the adjacent suburb of Clifton Beach has a medical centre, dentist and pathology lab. The closest hospital is Cairns Base Hospital about 35mins drive south in the Cairns CBD.

Shopping

Palm Cove has a number of convenience stores, eateries, bars and fashion stores. The nearest shopping centre is at Clifton Beach, about 5mins drive south, with a supermarket, post office, banks, a newsagent and specialty stores including furniture and homewares stores. The closest large shopping centre is at Smithfield, about 20mins drive south.

Childcare

There is no child care at Palm Cove but there is a large child care centre at Clifton Beach, 5mins drive south, and more options at Kewarra Beach, Trinity Beach and Smithfield.

School

There is no school at Palm Cove. The surrounding suburbs of Kewarra Beach, Trinity Beach, Trinity Park and Smithfield offer a range of schooling options including private, Catholic primary and a state high school.

Public transport

Sunbus provides public transport to the area and taxis also service the area.
