How Does It Work?

What will my property sell for is a simple three step process.

Step 1

Ask for your free report

Click on "Get your free report" and fill out the requested details of your property. Tell us as much about your property as you can the more accurate you are the more accurate we are.

Step 2

Your report will be researched and produced

First we will send a confirmation of your request. With the use of national databases, real estate portals and local real estate professional, your report will be completed. It will include an assessment of your property, list of current properties on the market and what has recently sold. You will also get details of your suburbs demographic make-up and a history of market movements.

Step 3

Receive your report

Your free property report will be sent to you via email. This is an estimate of what your property will sell for on today's market it not a valuation. We recommend getting a valuation prepared by a Qualified Valuer before any action regarding one's property is undertaken.
